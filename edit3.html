One of my best friends described the style of this edit to be one that felt like an "Apple ad", and while I definitely hadn't had that in mind when I was creating this edit, I love that that was what she associated with it. This is a different style of edit than what I usually made because of my use of colours (primarily yellow) and other solid layers. The incorporation of lyrics within the edit was something that I had intentionally done to also draw focus to the choice of audio for this edit. I also chose the colour yellow because it is very bright, which I felt was very fitting for the soft tone of the edit, and because the character Alina Starkov is one with the power of light.

My favourite effect in this edit is this one as the main focus is the sliding transition, but to the rest of the grid, I manipulated the 3d rotation to add some weight to the effect. I also love how I highlighted the center clip of the grid by adding a yellow tint to the rest of the clips, and additionally I ensured that the overlay of the floating yellow circles lay behind the center clip, adding dimensionality to this transition.
