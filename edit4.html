Another style that I don't usually do, this edit is one of my most unique style attempts (not in any way my style, a lot of inspiration from other accounts and edits were taken). Immediately, the intro manipulates the z-distance of the numbers as they come on screen, which is something I hadn't tried before. To blend each transition in this edit, I used a special blur effect using the BCC plugin for After Effects, adding a softer feel. Also in this edit I've used more solid layers, either in the background or within a transition and maintained a light blue and white theme. I'm particularly proud of the clock I created in the part where the lyrics say "time to". I masked the clip to show the lyrics at that moment, and within the 'O' I added clock hands to further emphasize the lyrics.
