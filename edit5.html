As I was making this edit, I remember that the transitions just flowed out of me so easily, I didn't take the time I usually do to brainstorm what I should do next. The tempo of the audio is faster than what I usually do, meaning that I had to find a good balance between having faster transitions and making sure the clips could actually be seen, which is how I came up with the transitions that acted on the same clip, so I could still try out different effects and highlight the current clip at the same time. I played with the lyrics showing up on screen on-beat, and adding overlay effects between the text and the clip so that there was still some change you could see onscreen.
